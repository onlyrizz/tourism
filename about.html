Welcome to Dinagat Island & A Hidden Paradise in the Philippines.

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Dinagat Island, where emerald landscapes, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant local culture await.

  • Pristine Beaches: White sands and turquoise seas perfect for swimming and island-hopping.
  • Natural Wonders: Limestone cliffs, lush forests, and enchanting caves to explore.
  • Cultural Richness: Warm hospitality and local traditions that celebrate island life.
  • Adventure Awaits: From kayaking to hiking scenic trails, Dinagat is a playground.
  • Best Time: November to May for sunny skies and calm seas.
  • Location: Dinagat Islands, Caraga Region, Philippines.
  • Activities: Eco-tours, beach escapes, and photography.

Welcome to Dinagat Island, a hidden paradise in the Philippines. With its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush landscapes, the island offers a perfect escape for travelers seeking both adventure and serenity.

Explore vibrant marine life, discover enchanting caves, and immerse yourself in the warmth of local culture. Whether you’re here to relax on the shore or hike scenic trails, Dinagat Island promises moments of wonder.
